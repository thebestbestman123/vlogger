Welcome to Vlogger!

Why Vlogger?


These days vlogging has become a sensation on the internet.
So far there's only one place to put those vlogs: YouTube.
Ways YouTube is kinda inefficient for vlog making:
  1. There's a whole bunch of other videos unrelated to vlogging
  2. You can't get your voice heard easily
  3. Most people make another channel for vlogging.
    Problem is that the channel won't get more views than it wants to.
  4. It's not made for vlogging but this website IS!!
So switch over to vlogger and get your voice heard!

How do I use Vlogger?

Easy! Just go to the signup page, signup for an account, and post your vlog!
